# BHV Research Group

Welcome to the **official GitHub organization of the BHV Research Group**.

The BHV Research Group creates solutions for advanced human–machine interaction, covering a wide spectrum of technologies, from brain–computer interfaces and digital health to virtual and augmented reality.

---

## Research Areas

### 🧠 Brain

Investigating the intricacies of the brain and augmenting the capabilities of human beings towards an improved quality of life through **EEG-based technologies**.

### 🩺 Health

Delivering **digital biomarkers** for the early detection of **neurodegenerative disorders**, using **wearables and IoT sensors** to capture behavioral patterns.

### 🥽 Virtual Reality

Exploring the challenges of **immersive technologies** for creative and meaningful **virtual worlds** across the wide spectrum of socioeconomic life.
